## **Your Discord server deserves an adventure.**



QuestCord turns any Discord community into a living, breathing RPG world. Complete daily quests. Battle massive bosses with your friends. Duel other players. Climb the global leaderboard. All without leaving Discord.



*And it's completely free.*

## What makes QuestCord different?



**Real interactive challenges** - Not just clicking buttons. Solve puzzles, test your reflexes, and prove your skills.



**Cross-server gameplay** - Travel virtually between Discord servers to find new quests and adventures.



**Live world events** - When a boss spawns, the whole community rallies together to take it down.



**Competitive PVP** - Challenge other players to 1v1 duels and build your combat record.



**Full progression system** - Level up, earn currency, buy legendary gear, and dominate the leaderboard.



**Real-time web dashboard** - Track global stats and leaderboards at **https://questcord.fun**

## The Quest System



Every server offers **5 daily quests**. But here's the twist - each quest throws a random challenge at you:



**Word Scramble** - Unscramble the word before time runs out

**Math Challenge** - Solve equations under pressure

**Trivia** - Test your knowledge

**Reaction Test** - Click when it turns green

**Memory Game** - Remember the emoji sequence



Choose from **Easy, Medium, or Hard** difficulty. Higher risk = higher rewards.



Success? Earn **Dakari** currency and **Gems**.

Failure? That quest is locked until tomorrow.



**Commands:**

`/quests` - View available quests

`/tutorial` - Learn how to play



---



## Travel the Discord Universe



Why limit yourself to one server? Use `/travel` to journey between opted-in communities and discover new quests.



Each journey takes **5-30 minutes** in real time. Plan your route wisely.



---



## World Boss Battles



When a boss appears, you have **60 minutes** to defeat it with your community.



Use `/attack` to deal damage. Use `/boss` to check its health.



The top damage dealer earns a **50% bonus** on all rewards. Think you can claim it?



Rewards include massive amounts of Dakari, Gems, and experience points.

## PVP Arena Combat



Enable PVP with `/pvp toggle` and challenge other players to duels.



Your **equipped gear** and **combat stats** determine the winner. Build your win-loss record and prove you're the strongest.



**Commands:**

`/pvp challenge @user` - Challenge someone to a duel

`/pvp stats` - View your PVP record
